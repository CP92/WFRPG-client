How to play

  • WASD to move your character
  • E to perform actions such as mine for gold or access the statue shop, and open chests.
  • Space bar to attack monsters
  • F to eat food once you have aquired some

Features

  • Sign-up/Sign-in
  • Autosave feature. Every 60 seconds the game will save, and upon loading the game your save will be loaded if one exists or created if it doesn't.
  • A lone chest (complete with chest animation and UI updates) that grants the player the pickaxe item and the ability to mine for gold. (to the right of the statue)
  • A shop statue which allows you to purchase food and upgrade your mining and strength levels.
  • Food will heal you when you've taken damage, and mining and strength levels make it easier and more profitable to mine and fight.
  • You can fight skellies to the left of the statue shop. Killing skellies will give you more gold.
